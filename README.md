# Adventure Works Sales Dashboard

## Recommended Structure and Order

### 1. Project Title / Headline

📊 **Adventure Works Sales Dashboard**

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Adventure Works sales data, providing insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales trends.

---

### 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Adventure Works Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to help business stakeholders monitor sales performance and identify growth opportunities. The dashboard enables users to analyze revenue, profit, customer performance, product sales, regional trends, and monthly sales through dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

### 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:<br>

• 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.<br>

• 📂 **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and data preparation.<br>

• 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic reporting.<br>

• 📝 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between sales, customers, products, and geography tables.<br>

• 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for dashboard development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

---

### 4. Data Source

**Source:** Microsoft Adventure Works Sales Dataset.

The dataset contains historical sales transactions, customer information, product details, sales territories, order history, and profit data. The data was cleaned, transformed, and modeled using Power Query before developing the dashboard.

---

### 5. Features / Highlights

The dashboard focuses on analyzing overall business performance and sales trends.

• **Business Problem**

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data, making it difficult to monitor performance and identify key growth opportunities. Questions such as:

- Which regions generate the highest sales?
- Who are the top-performing customers?
- Which products contribute the most revenue?
- How do sales vary by month, quarter, and year?
- What is the overall business profit?

are difficult to answer using raw transactional data.

• **Goal of the Dashboard**

To develop an interactive dashboard that:

- Monitors key business KPIs.
- Tracks sales and profit performance.
- Identifies top customers and products.
- Analyzes regional and time-based sales trends.
- Supports data-driven business decisions.

• **Walkthrough of Key Visuals**

- **Key KPIs (Top Section)**
  - Total Sales
  - Total Profit
  - Total Orders

- **Year-wise Sales (Column Chart)**
  Compares annual sales performance across different years.

- **Quarter-wise Sales (Pie Chart)**
  Displays the contribution of each quarter to total sales.

- **Top 10 Customers (Bar Chart)**
  Identifies customers generating the highest sales revenue.

- **Region Performance (Bar Chart)**
  Compares sales performance across different sales regions.

- **Month-wise Sales Trend (Line Chart)**
  Shows monthly sales trends to identify seasonal demand patterns.

- **Sales vs Production Cost (Combo Chart)**
  Compares sales amount with production cost across years.

- **Top 10 Products (Bar Chart)**
  Highlights the best-performing products based on sales.

- **Interactive Slicers**
  Allows users to filter the dashboard by:
  - Year
  - Quarter
  - Month

• **Business Impact & Insights**

- Identified top-performing regions contributing the highest revenue.
- Recognized high-value customers for targeted business strategies.
- Analyzed monthly and quarterly sales trends for better forecasting.
- Compared sales and production costs to evaluate profitability.
- Enabled faster and data-driven business decisions through interactive analytics.
